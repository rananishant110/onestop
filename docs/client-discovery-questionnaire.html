Meeting Date:

A. Business Overview
  1. Tell us about your business.
  2. What products do you sell?
  3. Who are your typical customers?
  4. Which locations or regions do you serve?
  5. What are your main business goals for this app?
  6. What problems are you trying to solve with this app?
B. Current Order Process
  1. How do customers place orders today?
  2. How are orders managed internally today?
  3. What is working well in the current process?
  4. What are the biggest pain points?
C. Customers and User Accounts
  1. Who will use the app?
  2. Will customers be businesses, individuals, or both?
  3. Does each customer account need multiple users or logins?
  4. Should new customers be able to register themselves?
  5. Do customer accounts need manual approval?
  6. Will different customers have different pricing?
D. Product Catalog
  1. Approximately how many products will be listed?
  2. How should products be organized?
  3. Do products have variants such as size, weight, flavor, or pack quantity?
  4. Do you have product photos and descriptions ready?
  5. Do you need SKU or barcode support?
  6. Do you sell by unit, case, carton, or pallet?
  7. Are minimum order quantities required?
E. Pricing
  1. Is pricing the same for all customers?
  2. Do some customers have custom pricing?
  3. Do you need tiered or volume pricing?
  4. Do prices change frequently?
  5. Should pricing only be visible after login?
  6. Do you offer discounts, promotions, or bundle deals?
F. Ordering Workflow
  1. What should the ordering process look like from start to finish?
  2. Should customers be able to:
  3. Do you allow backorders?
  4. Should substitute products be suggested when items are unavailable?
  5. Do you require order approval before confirmation?
G. Delivery / Pickup
  1. Do you offer delivery, pickup, or both?
  2. What areas do you deliver to?
  3. Do customers choose delivery dates or times?
  4. Do you have route-based delivery schedules?
  5. How are delivery charges calculated?
  6. Should customers be able to track order status?
H. Payments and Billing
  1. How do customers pay today?
  2. Do you want online payments in the app?
  3. Which payment methods do you want to support?
  4. Do customers have payment terms such as Net 15 or Net 30?
  5. Do you need credit limits per customer?
  6. Should customers see invoices, statements, and payment history?
I. Inventory and Internal Systems
  1. How do you currently track inventory?
  2. Do you use any software today?
  3. Should the app sync with inventory or accounting systems?
  4. Do you need real-time stock visibility?
J. Admin and Staff Features
  1. Who on your team will manage the system?
  2. Do you need different staff roles?
  3. Do sales reps need to place orders on behalf of customers?
  4. What reports do you need?
  5. Do you need alerts for:

L. Branding and Design
  1. Do you already have branding guidelines?
  2. Do you have a logo, colors, fonts, and brand assets?
  3. Do you have examples of apps or websites you like?
  4. Do you want a simple functional design or a premium custom design?
M. Timeline and Launch
  1. When do you want to launch?
  2. Is there a hard deadline?
  3. Do you want an MVP first?
  4. What features are must-have for launch?
  5. What features can wait until phase 2?
N. Budget and Engagement
  1. What budget range do you have in mind?
  2. Are you looking for:
  3. Who is the final decision-maker?
  4. How quickly can feedback and approvals be provided?
O. Maintenance and Support
  1. Do you need ongoing maintenance after launch?
  2. Do you need hosting and deployment included?
  3. Do you need training for your staff?
  4. Who will manage product updates, pricing updates, and customer accounts after launch?
Tip: Print this document to PDF or bring it to your client meeting and fill it out live. This questionnaire is designed to help define scope, identify MVP features, and avoid missing critical business requirements.
